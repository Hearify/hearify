## Common Grammar Mistakes This Quiz Covers

**Comma splice:** "I went to the store, I bought milk." ❌
→ "I went to the store. I bought milk." or "I went to the store, and I bought milk." ✓

**Its vs It's:** "The dog wagged it's tail." ❌
→ "The dog wagged its tail." ✓ (it's = it is; its = belonging to it)

**Who vs Whom:** "Who did you speak to?" ❌ (informal but accepted)
→ "To whom did you speak?" ✓ (whom = object pronoun, like him/her)

**Fewer vs Less:** "There are less people here today." ❌
→ "There are fewer people here today." ✓ (fewer = countable nouns; less = uncountable)
